I'll Ping You Pong

Your serve!

Here's how it works:

Enter a number in the field below, and I will count up to that number from 1. But there are a few Exceptions.

  1. Numbers that are divisible by 3 will be replaced with "ping."
  2. Numbers that are divisible by 5 will be replaced with "pong."
  3. Numbers that are divisible by both 3 and 5 will be replaced with "ping-pong."
